About Egypt

Egypt is a transcontinental country located in North Africa and Western Asia. Known for its ancient civilization, it boasts iconic landmarks such as the Pyramids of Giza, Sphinx, and the Nile River. With a rich history spanning thousands of years, Egypt is famous for its cultural heritage, including hieroglyphics, pharaohs, and mummies. Its capital is Cairo, and the country has a diverse landscape encompassing deserts, the Nile Delta, and the Red Sea coastline. Egypt's economy relies heavily on tourism, agriculture, and the Suez Canal, a vital trade route linking the Mediterranean Sea to the Red Sea.

Best places to visit in egypt

Pyramids of Giza: Marvel at the last surviving wonder of the ancient world, including the Great Pyramid of Khufu, the Sphinx, and the pyramids of Khafre and Menkaure.

Luxor: Explore the Valley of the Kings, Karnak Temple, Luxor Temple, and the Colossi of Memnon. Luxor is often referred to as an open-air museum due to its abundance of ancient Egyptian monuments.

Aswan: Visit the beautiful Philae Temple, take a boat ride on the Nile to see the Elephantine Island, and see the High Dam, which has created Lake Nasser.
